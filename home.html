About HARR

HARR (Holistic Academic Route Recommender) is a smart web application that helps students choose their ideal academic stream after the 10th grade. By analyzing recent academic performance, RIASEC, MBTI, Gardner’s Intelligence, and more, HARR provides tailored guidance to shape your future.

How It Works

1. Take Tests

Complete psychometric and academic assessments tailored to your strengths and interests.

2. Analyze Results

Our system evaluates your academic trends and personality data using advanced algorithms.

3. Get Recommendations

Receive personalized academic stream and career path recommendations to help you succeed.

Why Choose HARR?

Psychometric Tests

Includes RIASEC, MBTI, and Gardner to evaluate your personality, interests, and strengths.

Academic Insights

Analyzes your last three years’ academic records to identify consistent strengths and improvements.

Holistic Recommendations

Combines all data types to suggest the most suitable academic streams and degree paths for your future.
